16x16 overworld map tiles in the style of LPC. Commissioned by castelonia for the game Herodom.

The grass and water are based on tiles drawn by Lanea Zimmerman (AKA Sharm) for the original LPC assets. The sand and snow are based on tiles by Daniel Eddeland, William.Thompsonj, Nushio, and Adrix89.

Included in this set:

Terrain (grass, sand/desert, snow, water)
Forests (regular and snow)
Mountains
Hills (grass, desert, snow)
Paths (dirt and stone)
Rivers
Rocks (regular, desert, snow, water)
Houses, Towers, and Towns/Castles (regular, desert, snow)
Ship and Sailboat
Shipwreck
Docks
3 Animated Sea Creatures (dolphin, octopus, sea serpent)
4 Animated Banners (20 colors each)
2 Banner Patterns (20 colors each)
6 Panner Symbols (20 colors each)
