Numerous fruit-bearing trees, in the style of the Liberated Pixel Cup. Each tree includes 4 growth stages, plus three fruit-bearing stages. Some similar fruits share the same growth stages and tree shape (e.g. lemon/lime/orange), but have distinct fruiting frames. A few trees also have bare trunks or seasonal variants, but I don't have plans to finish those at the moment.

Works well with my food set, where you can find piles of these fruits to sell at market, or my crops set, where you can find other crops for your farm.

List of trees: apple x5, lemon, lime, tangerine, orange, peach, mango x2, cherry, pear x3, avocado, plum, coconut, banana.
